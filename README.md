# PowerBI-Financial-Performance-Dashboard
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing financial performance, profitability, ROI, compliance, fraud risk, credit risk, and market volatility across 8,900+ financial transactions.

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes financial performance, profitability, operational efficiency, and enterprise risk metrics across 8,900+ financial transactions.

The dashboard was designed to help business leaders monitor key financial indicators, identify risk exposure, and evaluate performance across regions and departments.

## Executive Dashboard

### Key Performance Indicators

- Total Revenue: $4.67 Billion
- Total Net Profit: $1.36 Billion
- Average ROI: 18.56%
- Average Gross Margin: 37.86%

### Business Insights

- Revenue generation remained relatively balanced across all regions.
- Investment and Audit departments generated the highest net profits.
- Average ROI remained consistent across regions.
- Over 77% of transactions achieved an "Excellent" financial status.

---

## Risk Analysis Dashboard

### Risk Metrics Analyzed

- Credit Risk Level
- Fraud Risk Score
- Compliance Score
- Market Volatility Index
- Financial Status Distribution

### Key Findings

- Average Fraud Risk Score: 0.50
- Average Credit Risk Level: 0.50
- Average Compliance Score: 80.03
- Market volatility remained relatively stable across regions.
- Compliance performance remained strong across departments.
